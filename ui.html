Saved sessions

Sessions are saved locally in your browser. Export to CSV for analysis in Excel/Google Sheets/Python. Clearing browser data will delete these.

Target values

Set the values you want to hit. A putt hits when every metric below is within its tolerance; the per-metric score is 100 × (1 − |dev|/tol) and the putt's overall score is the mean of the individual metric scores.

Calibrate offsets

Device-side calibration writes the zero reference into the sensor itself (same bytes the official Vertex app sends). The sensor stores it until you remove the device from the putter. Do this any time you re-insert the sensor.

Step 1 Face calibration
Lay the putter face-down, slightly raised above a flat surface (rest it on a book or the Vertex case). Face UP calibrates 180° backwards. Hold still and click Calibrate face.
—
Step 2 Lie calibration
Stand the putter against a wall so the top of the handle and the putter sole form a 90° angle (shaft vertical, head flat on the floor). Hold still and click Calibrate lie.
—
Sensor must be connected. Calibration commands are written to fff1; the sensor replies on fff1 once accepted.
